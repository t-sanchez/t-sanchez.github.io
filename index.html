About me

e-mail: firstname dot lastname at unil dot ch

I am working as a postdoctoral research at the Medical Image Analysis Laboratory with Mertixell Bach Cuadra, in the SP CHUV-UNIL unit of the Center for Biomedial Imaging (CIBM). My current project revolves around developing a deep learning-based quality control pipeline for fetal brain Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI).

I previously completed my PhD in Machine Learning at EPFL, Switzerland. I worked Laboratory for Information and Inference Systems (LIONS), under the supervision of Volkan Cevher. My research was focused on deep learning applied to medical imaging, mostly to problems of reconstruction and optimization of sampling trajectories for MRI.

I am also broadly interested by generative models, inverse problems, variational inference and experiment design.
